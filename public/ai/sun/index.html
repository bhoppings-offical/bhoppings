Settings

Instructions

Use the controls above to customize the animation:

  • Adjust sliders for sun speed, wave speed, wave intensity, and color viscosity.
  • Toggle options to cycle wave speed and intensity over time.
  • Select from various color schemes to change the visual theme.
  • Switch between different celestial objects: Sun, Full Moon, or Black Hole.
  • Use custom colors to create your unique gradient.

You can toggle the settings panel by moving your mouse to reveal the settings button at the top right corner. The settings panel will automatically hide after 5 seconds of inactivity.
