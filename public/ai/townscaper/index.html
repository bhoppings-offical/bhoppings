Welcome to Townscaper!

About the Game:
Townscaper is a creative building game where you can create beautiful, colorful towns with no goal other than to build and enjoy.

How to Play:

  • Left click to place blocks
  • Right click to remove blocks
  • Mouse wheel to change colors
  • Hold middle mouse button to pan
  • Hold right mouse button to rotate
  • Ctrl + Z to undo

Tips & Tricks:

  • Blocks automatically form into houses, arches, and stairs based on their placement
  • Try building in different patterns to discover unique architectural features
  • Experiment with color combinations to create different moods
  • You can build underwater and create floating structures
